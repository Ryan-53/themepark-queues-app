About Themepark Queues App


Helpful tool for themepark goers
Enables users to view the wait times for themepark rides.
Unique Features
  • Subscribe for email notifications of ride reopening when they are closed (only available once logged in).
    		•
    Future Features
  • Subscribe for email notifications if a rides wait time goes below a specified point.
    		•
  • ML models will predict how the ride times will change so you can beat the queues.
    		•
  • Other themeparks will be added, allowing the user to pick which themepark to look at.
    		•
  • A user will be able to favourite certain rides to more quickly find it to track its queue time.
